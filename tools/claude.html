About Claude

Claude is a family of large language models developed by Anthropic. Designed to be helpful, honest, and harmless, Claude focuses on safety and steerability. It is known for its large context window, allowing it to process entire books or large codebases in a single prompt, and for its natural, nuanced conversation style.

Key Features

  • Large Context Window: Can process up to 200,000+ tokens, enabling analysis of long documents.
  • Safety Focus: Built with Constitutional AI to reduce harmful outputs and hallucinations.
  • Advanced Reasoning: Strong capabilities in complex reasoning, math, and coding tasks.
  • Natural Conversation: Designed to be more conversational and less robotic in its responses.
