About Google Gemini

Gemini is a family of multimodal AI models developed by Google DeepMind. Unlike other models trained primarily on text, Gemini was built from the ground up to be multimodal, meaning it can seamlessly understand, operate across, and combine different types of information including text, code, audio, image, and video.

Key Features

  • Native Multimodality: Can process and generate text, images, audio, and video natively.
  • Google Integration: seamlessly integrates with Google Workspace apps like Docs, Gmail, and Drive.
  • Advanced Coding: excels at competitive programming and complex coding tasks.
  • Real-time Information: Connected to Google Search for up-to-date information.
